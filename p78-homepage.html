The thesis

The next decade of space will be defined not by what gets launched — but by what gets reused, repositioned, and reached.

Why P78

Stations and constellations need to be serviced. The Moon needs to be worked, not just visited. None of that happens without the vehicles to make it routine.

P78 is building that backbone — the connective infrastructure that turns space from a series of one-shot deployments into a working economy reaching all the way to the Moon.
